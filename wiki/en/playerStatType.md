# Player statistics type

Usage ```PlayerStatType::of(PlayerStatType::PVP)```

Available options:
* PVE - PvE
* PVP - PvP
* GATHERING - Gathering
* CRAFTING - Crafting
 
# Player statistics subtype
 
Usage ```PlayerStatSubType::of(PlayerStatSubType::ORE)```

Available options:
* ALL - All types
* FIBER - Fiber
* HIDE - Hide
* ORE - Ore
* ROCK - Rock
* WOOD - Wood
